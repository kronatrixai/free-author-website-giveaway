# KRONATRIX Author Email Capture Minimal Site

Repo title: `kronatrix-author-email-capture`

Repo description: Minimal KRONATRIX author lead capture page with logo, audio, and an email-based author application form. Authors enter their author name, contact email, book title, social profile URLs from dropdowns, optional Amazon book URL, and other named URLs. The CTA opens an email addressed to kronatrix.ai@gmail.com so they can attach their book cover, full book interior PDF / manuscript, and any extra site images they want considered.

Target domain: `freeauthorwebsite.kronatrix.co.uk`

Main files:
- `index.html`
- `404.html`
- `CNAME`
- `robots.txt`
- `sitemap.xml`
- `llms.txt`
- `llms-full.txt`
- `logo.webp`
- `author-ai-chat-voiceover.mp3`
- favicon and social preview images

Key lead-capture flow:
1. Author listens to the audio.
2. Author enters the author name they want used on the website.
3. Author adds social links through dropdown rows.
4. Author optionally adds Amazon and other website/book links.
5. CTA opens email to `kronatrix.ai@gmail.com`.
6. Author attaches the book cover image and full PDF of the book interior / manuscript.
7. Author can also attach extra site images such as author photos, book character images, logos, or other book visuals.
8. Selected sites usually take up to 48 hours; KRONATRIX emails the author back when completed with the finished page details and anything needed moving forward.

Privacy note shown on site: KRONATRIX does not share or publish the submitted PDF. It is used privately to understand the book, themes, audience, and what is needed for the author website.
